Bloom

Goal Setting & Progress Tracking Web App

It can be hard to keep track of all our goals for the different things in our lives whether it
be fitness, education, or our careers. Bloom is a way for you to plan goals, and add subgoals
along the way. You can filter through goals by priority to stay organized, post progress updates,
and upload photos that you can look back on when you've accomplished your goal!

Visit: https://blooming.herokuapp.com
Github: https://github.com/Hunter-College-CSCI-39548-Fall-2018/team2
Stack: React, Node/Express, MongoDB

Food Junkies

Food Discovery Android App

Food Junkies is the answer to the age old question "Where should we eat?"
We recommend dishes to you based on your preferences, and the more you use the app
the more personalized the recommendations are! Swipe through a short cuisine quiz so
we know what kinds of dishes to start you off with. After swiping through and finding a dish
you like we'll find the top restraunts in your area based on rating, price, and distance!

Github: https://github.com/kahhauyap/FoodJunkies
Stack: Java, MySQL

Fict

Health & Fitness Nutrition Tracker Android App

Traditional calorie trackers suffer from the tedious task of having to manually record foods
and their nutrition. Fict gives you the option of taking a picture of your meal, then using
Visual Recognition classify the dish and return nutritional information for easy logging.
Create personalized diet plans with the Macronutrition Calculator, and get started!

Github: https://github.com/kahhauyap/Fict
Stack: Java, SQLite
